Speaker Profiles

🎤 Prof. Chien-Yeh Hsu

Prof. Hsu is the President of Taiwan Association for Medical Informatics and a leading expert in health informatics. He has published over 150 papers in peer-reviewed journals and conferences. His research focuses on the application of AI and machine learning in healthcare systems, clinical decision support systems, and health information exchange.

Key Contributions: Founded the Medical Informatics Lab at his institution, developed several clinical decision support systems adopted by hospitals across Asia.

🎤 Prof. Volker B. Schulte

Prof. Schulte is a leading figure in European health informatics and privacy-preserving technologies. With a PhD in Computer Science, he has worked extensively on secure health data processing and federated learning for medical applications. He is a frequent speaker at international conferences and has contributed to several healthcare IT standards.

Key Contributions: Developed frameworks for privacy-preserving healthcare AI, consulted for multiple European healthcare organizations on GDPR compliance in AI systems.

🎤 Prof. Jim-Tong Horng

Prof. Horng is a distinguished professor at Chang Gung University and a pioneer in bioinformatics and clinical data analytics. His research has contributed significantly to the understanding of genomic data patterns and their clinical applications. He has led numerous research collaborations with medical centers across Asia.

Key Contributions: Developed AI algorithms for disease prediction from genomic data, published over 200 research papers in leading international journals.

🎤 Prof. Peter Chang

Prof. Chang is the Chief Medical Information Officer at Show Chwan Healthcare System and Chairman of Taiwan Association of Healthcare Leaders. With both MD and MBA degrees, he brings a unique perspective on healthcare management and digital transformation. He has led successful digital transformation initiatives in multiple healthcare organizations.

Key Contributions: Implemented enterprise-wide health information systems, led digital transformation strategy for major hospital networks in Asia.
