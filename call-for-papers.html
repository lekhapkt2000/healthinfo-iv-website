Call for Papers

Submit your research to HEALTHINFO IV 2026

Paper Submission Guidelines

We welcome original research papers, case studies, and practical applications

Paper Length

  • Full Paper: 6–10 pages (including references)
  • Short Paper: 4–6 pages (including references)
  • Format: Microsoft Word or LaTeX
  • Margins: 1 inch (2.54 cm) on all sides
  • Font: Times New Roman, 12pt
  • Line Spacing: Single-spaced

Review Process

  • Review Method: Double-Blind Peer Review
  • Reviewers: Minimum 2 expert reviewers per paper
  • Timeline: 6-8 weeks for review and decision
  • Criteria: Scientific merit, novelty, clarity, and applicability

Publication

  • Accepted papers will be published in the Conference Proceedings
  • Papers will be indexed in major academic databases
  • Selected high-quality papers may be published in partner journals
  • All authors must present their papers at the conference

Conference Topics

Submit papers related to the following research areas

🏥

Health Equity

AI-driven telemedicine to bridge the gap between urban centers and rural provinces, ensuring no citizen is left behind.

🛡️

Health Protection

Advanced predictive modeling to detect outbreaks and environmental threats before they become crises.

⚙️

Healthcare Management

Clinical decision support systems that empower doctors with precision data for accurate diagnosis and treatment.

🔄

Health Rehabilitation & Restoration

The integration of robotics and intelligent monitoring to help patients reclaim their lives after illness or injury.

📚

Health Education

Interactive, AI-enhanced training for individuals and communities to improve their health by increasing knowledge, influencing motivation, and improving health literacy.

📖

Health Literacy

Breaking down the barriers of “doctor language” by using AI to translate complex medical data into clear, actionable knowledge that enables people to access, understand, appraise, and use information and services in ways that promote and maintain good health and well-being for themselves and those around them.

💪

Health Promotion

Personalized AI wellness coaching that encourages proactive lifestyles, enables people to increase control over, and to improve their health.

How to Submit

Follow these steps to submit your paper

Step 1: Prepare Your Paper

Follow the paper format guidelines and download the template. Ensure your paper includes:

  • Title (clear and concise)
  • Author names and affiliations
  • Abstract (150-200 words)
  • Keywords (5-7 terms)
  • Introduction, methodology, results, and conclusion
  • References (IEEE or Harvard style)

Step 2: Online Submission

Submit your paper through our online submission system:

  • Visit: www.healthinfo-iv.com/submit
  • Create an account or log in
  • Select the research topic
  • Upload your paper (PDF format)
  • Fill in author information

Step 3: Confirmation

You will receive:

  • Confirmation email with submission ID
  • Acknowledgment of receipt
  • Timeline for review process
  • Notification of acceptance/rejection (6-8 weeks)

⚠️ Important Notes

  • Submission deadline: July 15, 2026
  • Late submissions will not be accepted
  • All authors must present their papers at the conference
  • At least one author must register before the author registration deadline
  • Papers must be original and not previously published
