Frequently Asked Questions

When is the paper submission deadline?

The paper submission deadline is July 15, 2026. Late submissions will not be accepted.

Can I present my paper remotely?

Yes, online participation options are available. However, we encourage at least one author to be present onsite for the live Q&A session.

What is the registration fee for students?

Student registration fees are offered at a discounted rate. Please refer to the Registration page for full details.

Do I need a visa to enter Vietnam?

Visa requirements vary by nationality. Please consult the official Vietnam Immigration Portal or your nearest Vietnamese Embassy. We can provide an official invitation letter upon request to support your visa application.

Is accommodation provided during the conference?

Accommodation is not included with conference registration. Please visit the Venue & Travel page for recommended hotels and estimated rates near the venue.

When will the final conference program be published?

The final program will be published on this website by September 15, 2026, and will be available for all registered participants.
