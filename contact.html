Frequently Asked Questions

When is the paper submission deadline?

The paper submission deadline is July 15, 2026. Late submissions will not be accepted.

Can I present my paper remotely?

Yes, we offer online participation options. However, at least one author should be present for the live Q&A session if possible.

What is the registration fee for students?

Student registration fees are: Early bird ($200), Regular ($250), and On-site ($300). Valid student ID is required.

Do I need a visa to enter Vietnam?

It depends on your nationality. Check the Vietnamese Immigration website for specific requirements. We can provide an invitation letter if needed.

Is accommodation provided during the conference?

Accommodation is not included with conference registration, but we offer discounted rates at partner hotels. Contact us for details.

Can I download the conference program before the event?

Yes, the final program will be published on our website by September 15, 2026, and will be available for registered participants.
