About HEALTHINFO IV

Learn more about the conference, its history, and organizers

What is HEALTHINFO?

HEALTHINFO is an international conference series focused on health informatics and digital transformation in healthcare. The series has been established to bring together leading researchers, healthcare professionals, academicians, and industry experts from around the world.

Conference History

  • HEALTHINFO 2019 - 1st edition, launched in Vietnam
  • HEALTHINFO 2022 - 2nd edition, expanded international participation
  • HEALTHINFO 2023 - 3rd edition, hybrid format introduced
  • HEALTHINFO IV 2026 - 4th edition, AI for Healthcare focus

HEALTHINFO IV Highlights

  • Theme: AI for Healthcare
  • Date: October 02–03, 2026
  • Venue: University of Information Technology, VNU-HCM
  • Format: Hybrid (Onsite + Online)
  • Expected Participants: 150+
  • Research Papers: 50+
  • Keynote Speakers: 4
  • Research Topics: 7

Conference Objectives

HEALTHINFO IV aims to achieve the following key objectives

🤝 Connect Researchers

Bring together researchers and academics working on health informatics and AI in healthcare.

🏥 Connect Healthcare Professionals

Provide a platform for physicians, nurses, and healthcare managers to share practical experiences.

🏢 Connect Organizations

Facilitate networking between hospitals, universities, and healthcare IT companies.

📚 Share Research Results

Publish and present cutting-edge research in health informatics and medical AI.

💡 Share Practical Applications

Showcase real-world implementations and case studies from healthcare organizations.

🚀 Promote Innovation

Encourage discussion and collaboration on innovative solutions for healthcare challenges.
