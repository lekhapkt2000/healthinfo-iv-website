Registration

Register to attend HEALTHINFO IV 2026

Registration Categories

There is no registration fee for participating in the conference.

Registration Includes

✅ Conference Access

Attend all keynote sessions, parallel tracks, and networking events throughout the conference.

✅ Conference Materials

Receive the conference program, book of abstracts, and welcome bag with event materials.

✅ Coffee & Meals

Complimentary coffee breaks and catered lunches on both conference days.

✅ Welcome Gala Dinner

Enjoy the Welcome Gala Dinner on October 02 with keynote speakers and fellow participants.

✅ Certificate

Receive a certificate of attendance for participating in the conference.

✅ Networking

Access to networking platform connecting researchers and professionals worldwide.

How to Register

Step 1: Create Account

Visit our online registration portal and create an account with your email address.

Go to Registration Portal

Step 2: Fill Your Profile

Complete your profile with your personal information, institution, and contact details.

Step 3: Select Category

Choose your registration category (Professional, Student, Industry, Author, or Online).

Step 4: Make Payment

Choose your payment method:

  • 💳 Credit Card (Visa, Mastercard, American Express)
  • 🏦 Bank Transfer (Wire Transfer for international participants)
  • 📱 Mobile Payment (Zalo Pay, Momo for Vietnamese participants)

Step 5: Confirmation

You will receive a confirmation email with your registration details and receipt.

Important Information

Cancellation & Refund Policy

  • Cancellations received before September 15: Full refund minus 15% administrative fee
  • Cancellations between September 15-30: 50% refund
  • No refund for cancellations after September 30

Paper Authors

At least one author of each accepted paper must register to include the paper in the program. The author registration deadline is September 10, 2026.

Student Registration

Students must provide a valid student ID or letter from their institution confirming their student status.
