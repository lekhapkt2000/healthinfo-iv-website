About HEALTHINFO IV Conference

Connecting AI and Healthcare Innovation

The 4th International Health Informatics Conference (HEALTHINFO IV) is a premier international forum that brings together researchers, healthcare professionals, policymakers, and industry experts to explore the latest advances in health informatics and digital healthcare. Under the theme “AI for Healthcare,” the conference focuses on the transformative role of artificial intelligence in improving healthcare delivery, clinical practice, health management, and patient outcomes. HEALTHINFO IV aims to foster interdisciplinary collaboration, promote innovative research, and strengthen partnerships among academia, healthcare institutions, and industry worldwide.

Research Areas

Conference Tracks

The conference welcomes research and practical contributions across 7 key thematic tracks in AI and Healthcare.

🏥

Health Equity

AI-driven telemedicine to bridge the gap between urban centers and rural provinces, ensuring no citizen is left behind.

🛡️

Health Protection

Advanced predictive modeling to detect outbreaks and environmental threats before they become crises.

⚙️

Healthcare Management

Clinical decision support systems that empower doctors with precision data for accurate diagnosis and treatment.

🔄

Health Rehabilitation & Restoration

The integration of robotics and intelligent monitoring to help patients reclaim their lives after illness or injury.

📚

Health Education

Interactive, AI-enhanced training for individuals and communities to improve their health by increasing knowledge, influencing motivation, and improving health literacy.

📖

Health Literacy

Breaking down the barriers of “doctor language” by using AI to translate complex medical data into clear, actionable knowledge that enables people to access, understand, appraise, and use information and services in ways that promote and maintain good health and well-being for themselves and those around them.

💪

Health Promotion

Personalized AI wellness coaching that encourages proactive lifestyles, enables people to increase control over, and to improve their health.
