Conference Venue

  • ✓ Registration desk and information center

🗺️ Getting There

From Tan Son Nhat International Airport:

  • Distance: 8 km
  • Travel time: 15-30 minutes (depending on traffic)
  • Options: Taxi, Grab, Airport bus

By Taxi/Grab:

  • Estimated cost: 250,000 - 400,000 VND
  • Download Grab app for convenient booking

By Public Transport:

  • Bus Route 152, 49, or 64 to the University
  • Estimated cost: 10,000 VND

Local Transportation

🚕 Taxi & Ride-sharing

Grab App: Popular in Ho Chi Minh City, download the app for convenient booking.

Vinasun Taxi: Traditional taxi service, reliable for long distances.

Average cost: 15,000-30,000 VND per trip

🚌 Public Bus

Bus Routes: Route 152, 49, 64, and 21 connect to the university area.

Bus Cards: Purchase a rechargeable card at bus stations.

Cost: 10,000 VND per trip

🚲 Bicycle & Motorbike

Bike Rental: Available at hotels and rental shops.

Motorbike Rental: Popular in Vietnam, helmet required.

Cost: 50,000-200,000 VND per day

🚇 Metro (Coming Soon)

Ho Chi Minh City Metro Line 1 will connect the central area with District 5 in the future.

Currently under construction, expected completion: 2027

🛴 Electric Scooters

Grab Bike: E-scooter and motorbike taxi services available via Grab app.

Cost: 20,000-50,000 VND per trip

🏨 Airport Transfers

Conference organizers can arrange airport pickup. Contact info@healthinfo-iv.com to book.

Cost: $30-50 one-way

Visa Information for International Participants

📋 Visa Requirements

Citizens of many countries require a visa to enter Vietnam. Check the Vietnamese Immigration website for specific requirements based on your nationality.

Visa Types:

  • Tourist Visa (90 days): Most common for conference participants
  • Business Visa (1 year): For frequent travelers
  • E-visa: Available for many nationalities, can be applied online

📜 Invitation Letter

We can provide an official invitation letter from the conference organizers to support your visa application. Please contact us at:

Email: visa@healthinfo-iv.com

Include: Your full name, passport number, and intended travel dates
