Venue & Travel

Information about the conference venue, transportation, and accommodation

Conference Location

Conference Venue

University of Information Technology (UIT)

The 4th International Health Informatics Conference (HEALTHINFO IV) will be held at the University of Information Technology (UIT), a member university of Vietnam National University Ho Chi Minh City (VNU-HCM).

UIT is one of Vietnam's leading universities in information technology, providing modern conference facilities, advanced teaching and research infrastructure, and a dynamic academic environment for international collaboration.

Venue Address

  • University of Information Technology (UIT)
  • Vietnam National University Ho Chi Minh City (VNU-HCM)
  • Quarter 34, Linh Xuan Ward
  • Ho Chi Minh City, Vietnam

Facilities

  • Modern conference halls equipped with audiovisual systems
  • High-speed Wi-Fi throughout the venue
  • Accessible facilities and parking areas
  • On-campus dining and cafeteria services
  • Registration and information support desks

Getting to the Venue

From Tan Son Nhat International Airport (SGN)

  • Distance: approximately 22 km
  • Estimated travel time: 30–45 minutes (depending on traffic)

Travel options

  • Taxi services
  • Ride-hailing applications (Grab, Green SM, Be)
  • Public buses
  • Metro and connecting transportation

Estimated airport transfer:
200,000 – 350,000 VND by taxi or ride-hailing

Local Transportation

Transportation options available in Ho Chi Minh City

Taxi & Ride-Hailing

Widely available throughout the city. Recommended services include Grab, Green SM, Be, Vinasun Taxi, and Mai Linh Taxi.

Estimated airport transfer cost: 200,000–350,000 VND

Metro

Ho Chi Minh City Metro Line 1 (Ben Thanh – Suoi Tien) provides access between the city center and eastern districts. Connect to the venue via taxi or bus.

Check the latest metro schedules before traveling.

Public Bus

A number of public bus routes connect the city center, Thu Duc area, and the Vietnam National University campus.

Estimated fare: 7,000–14,000 VND per trip

Accommodation

Recommended options near the conference venue

Bcons City Hotel

Approximately 3.5 km from the venue

  • Estimated rate: from 600,000 VND per night

VNU-HCMC Guest House

Approximately 2 km from the venue — within VNU-HCM area

  • Estimated rate: from 300,000 VND per night

Participants are encouraged to make accommodation arrangements early. Additional recommendations may be announced closer to the conference date.

Visa Information

Entry Requirements

International participants may require a visa to enter Vietnam. Please consult the official Vietnam Immigration Portal and your nearest Vietnamese Embassy or Consulate for the most up-to-date visa requirements and application procedures based on your nationality.

Invitation Letter

Official invitation letters can be provided upon request after registration and acceptance confirmation to support visa applications.

Please contact: info@healthinfo-iv.com

Weather in October

October in Ho Chi Minh City is generally warm and humid, with average temperatures ranging from 24°C to 31°C.

Light clothing is recommended. Participants may also wish to carry an umbrella or light rain jacket, as occasional rainfall is possible during this period.

Explore Ho Chi Minh City

Ho Chi Minh City is Vietnam's largest economic and cultural center, offering a unique blend of history, innovation, cuisine, and modern urban life.

  • Ben Thanh Market

  • Independence Palace
  • Landmark 81

We Welcome All Participants

We warmly welcome all participants to HEALTHINFO IV and hope you enjoy both the conference and your stay in Ho Chi Minh City.
